About GRIS

We work in the Computer Science Department of the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro and in other requesting units of the University. Our objective is the detection, resolution and prevention of security incidents, in addition to offering academic support to UFRJ students who have a particular interest in the area of ​​information security. Among the main activities of GRIS are:

  • Apply security updates
  • Record and monitor security incidents
  • Evaluate network security conditions
  • Disseminate security practices and recommendations
  • Assist in repairing damage caused by security incidents
  • Analyze compromised systems looking for causes, damages and culprits (forensic analysis)
  • Provide education and training for system administrators

The main mission of GRIS, as a project of the Department of Computer Science, is to offer guidance and academic support to students of the Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science at the Department of Computer Science at UFRJ, a non-profit organization, with regard to the theme of Information Security in all its aspects, in the areas of teaching, research and extension, and this is the main, if not the only, guiding criterion for internal and external issues.
